Chocolate Cake

Descriptions

 This is that simple but very tasty and moisty chocolate cake. It is perfect to go with when you have a crowd who likes to stick with basics. Follow the instructions carefully and you're going to fall in love with this recipe.

Portions:12

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 1 cup of soy oil
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 cups of refined sugar
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 2 cups of cocoa powder
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • butter and granulated sugar enough to grease the pan

Steps

Cake
  1. Pre heat the oven at 180Â°C
  2. Grease the pan with the butter and add granulated sugar, only enough t stick to the butter
  3. In a blender, mix the eggs, the oil and the milk.
  4. Add the refined sugar and blend it again for more 2 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, place the flour, the cocoa powder and mix until a homogeneous mixture is obtained.
  6. To this mixture, add what was mixed in the blender and mix it by hand.
  7. Add the baking powder and mix kindly.
  8. Pour the cake into the pan and place it in the pre heated oven for 40 minutes.
  9. After 30 minutes, open the oven and pierce the middle of the cake with a fork. If the fork comes out dry, your cake is ready, if not, let more 10 minutes and check it again. DO NOT OPEN THE OVEN BEFORE 30 MINUTES.
  10. When ready, turn off the oven and let the cake cool off a little bit before getting it out of the pan. DO NOT TRY TO TAK THE CAKE OUT OF THE PAN WHILE IT'S TOO HOT.
Chocolate Sauce
  1. In a pan, add a tablespoon of butter, 1 can of condensed milk and 1/3 of a cup of chocolate powder.
  2. Mix over low heat to obtain a homogeneous mixture.
  3. When the mixture is coming off the bottom of the pan, turn off the heat.
  4. Once the cake is ready and already out of the pan, pour the chocolate sauce on top of the cake.
