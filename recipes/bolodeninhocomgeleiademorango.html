Strawberry Milk Cake

Descriptions

 This is a cake I came up with after having a similar one but not being able to find all the ingredients to buy. It camae out very different? Yes, for sure. But it also came out really good and all my friends loved it.

Portions:12

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 6 egg whites
  • 150g of butter
  • 1 cup and 3/4 of a cup of sugar
  • 2 cups and 1/4 of cup of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 3 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 2 cups of powdered milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 500g of strawberries
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 lime
  • butter and granulated sugar enough to grease the pan

Steps

Cake
  1. Pre heat the oven at 180Â°C
  2. Grease the pan with the butter and add granulated sugar, only enough t stick to the butter
  3. In a blender, mix the eggs whites, the milk, the refined sugar and the butter.
  4. In a bowl mix by hand the mixture, the vanilla extract and the flour until a homogeneous mixture is obtained.
  5. Add the baking powder and mix kindly.
  6. Pour the cake into the pan and place it in the pre heated oven for 30 minutes.
  7. After 30 minutes, open the oven and pierce the middle of the cake with a fork. If the fork comes out dry, your cake is ready, if not, let more 10 minutes and check it again. DO NOT OPEN THE OVEN BEFORE 30 MINUTES.
  8. When ready, turn off the oven and let the cake cool off a little bit before getting it out of the pan. DO NOT TRY TO TAK THE CAKE OUT OF THE PAN WHILE IT'S TOO HOT.
Milk ganache
  1. In a pan, place the condensed milk, the egg yolks, the milk and the powdered milk.
  2. Mix over low heat to obtain a homogeneous mixture.
  3. When the mixture starts to boil mix for more 5 minutes and turn off the heat.
Strawberry jam
  1. In a large pan, add the strawberries, sugar and the juice of one lime, and toss it all together.
  2. Bring to a boil, and cook while stirring until thick, about 10 to 12 minutes.
Setting up the cake
  1. Once your cake is out of the pan, cut it in half whith a sewing thread or dental floss not used before.
  2. Place on top of the first half the strawberry jam.
  3. Cover the jam with the other half like a sandwich.
  4. On top of the cake place the milk ganache.
  5. If you want, you can decorate the cake with some fresh strawberries, a little bit of powdered milk or make some drawing with the strawberry jam.
